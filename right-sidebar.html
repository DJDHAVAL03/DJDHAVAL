ABOUT ME

Harmonizing Passions, Engineering Excellence, and Creative Brilliance

Welcome to my world of diverse experiences and unwavering passion for excellence. I'm Dhaval Patel, a multi-talented individual with a penchant for exploring various facets of life and continuously evolving both personally and professionally.

A DJ with a Groove:

I kick-started my career as a DJ at Dhaval Entertainment and Event Services, where I channel my musical talents into crafting memorable experiences for audiences. The rhythm and beats are not just my passion; they're my way of connecting people through the universal language of music.

Automotive Enthusiast:

My journey into the automotive world began at Jackson Motor Company (JMC), where I delved into the intricate world of automobiles. Prior to that, I was a dedicated motor mechanic at Om Automotive, where I honed my skills and understanding of vehicle repair and maintenance. An internship at Parramatta Automotive Services further solidified my expertise.

Engineering Mindset:

I am a proud alumnus of A.D. Patel Institute of Technology, where I studied Automotive Engineering. My education has provided me with a strong technical foundation that continues to serve me well in various aspects of my life.

Visual Storyteller:

Intrigued by the world of visual merchandising, I ventured into the role of a Visual Merchandiser (VM), employing my creativity and attention to detail to create captivating displays.

Mastering Management:

Recognizing the importance of management skills in today's competitive landscape, I pursued a Master of Management for Engineers at CQUniversity Australia. This educational journey has equipped me with the knowledge and skills needed to thrive in a managerial capacity within the engineering realm.
